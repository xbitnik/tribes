Tribes X Live

From the legendary NFK engine that founded the online shooter genre comes the most exciting, energetic, and fast-paced 2D shooter on the Internet called Tribes X. Play against bots, monsters or players around the world in over 30 maps, and a dozen game modes, in one of the most competitive 2D shooters online today.

Custom maps

Make your way through over 30 high-quality maps, spanning more than a decade of the community's best creations. From the classics, to brand new author's maps, you'll be sure to find your favorite little camping spot somewhere there.

Hardcore game types

Frag every man, bot, and monster in Deathmatch game type, enjoy some classic Teamplay action or Capture the Flag, try some more modern modes like Domination, or Super Practice. Tribes X offers a mode for whatever mood you're in with a faster and dynamic physics.

Great community

Download new maps, gametype variations, player models, and more from our web-site. Find something new and cool every day courtesy of some of Tribes X and NFK most hardcore fans. Join our Discord channel and find someone to play with.
